Exercises for Mental Health & Yoga Practices

Mental Health Exercises

1. Deep Breathing

Practice deep breathing to reduce stress and anxiety. Breathe in for 4 counts, hold for 7 counts, and exhale for 8 counts.

2. Mindfulness Meditation

Focus on the present moment and observe your thoughts without judgment. This helps improve emotional well-being.

3. Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Tense and relax each muscle group in your body, starting from your toes and moving to your head, to relieve tension.
