Healthy Foods & Diets for Mental Health

Foods for Better Mental Health

1. Fatty Fish

Rich in omega-3 fatty acids, which are essential for brain health and can help reduce symptoms of depression.

2. Leafy Greens

Spinach, kale, and other greens are high in folate, which supports mood regulation and cognitive function.

3. Nuts and Seeds

Packed with healthy fats, magnesium, and antioxidants that promote brain health and reduce anxiety.
